## Please Take a Moment to Understand Before Reporting
We kindly ask that you take the time to understand our work before choosing to report it. Our mod was created for people including **children** who have gone through 
difficult or traumatic experiences early in life, when their minds were still learning how to understand the world.

Age Regression has an important place in healing and self-expression, especially in games where it’s easier to relax and act younger without the stigma of “acting your 
age.” I personally experienced several forms of abuse at a young age, and much of what I include in this project is based on genuine medical treatments I received 
growing up. The mod draws inspiration from the UK’s NHS healthcare system, while the term TAR (Therapeutic Age Regression) comes from US medical terminology but is 
still understood in the UK.

When you falsely report our mod, you’re not only causing unnecessary strain on the platform but you’re also taking away something that truly helps people. Yes, some 
of those people are children. Imagine a young person who wants to safely relive a comforting part of their childhood within Minecraft, because they can’t talk about 
it openly at school. When you report and remove that opportunity, you take away one of the few safe outlets they have.

Please don’t misjudge what we’re doing. Our project is not connected to the **ABDL**,**DDLG** and **Age Play** communities and any other Adult Related communities. I am incontinent 
myself and I don’t enjoy needing to wear protection, but I do believe it’s important to represent medical realities with dignity and not ridicule. Unfortunately, some 
groups treat these issues as jokes, which makes it harder for real people with medical needs to feel seen and respected.

In our mod, items like protection have practical in-game benefits (for example, slow falling), allowing players to relax and enjoy the experience without worry. That’s 
the purpose for comfort, inclusion, and gentle healing through play.
