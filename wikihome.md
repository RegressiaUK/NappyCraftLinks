### What Is NappyCraft ?
📖 **NappyCraft** is a Minecraft mod designed to support inclusive simulation play, comfort mechanics, and self-expression within the game. It introduces features that allow players to simulate the use of nappies/diapers in a way that reflects real-life experiences and supportive environments.

**The mod is built around two core goals**:

 - **Inclusivity for disabled players** – Some individuals use nappies/diapers in daily life for medical or accessibility reasons. **NappyCraft** provides representation within Minecraft, helping players feel acknowledged and included, and offering a space where their experiences can be approached with comfort or lightheartedness.

 - **Support for age regression as a coping mechanism** – For some people, age regression is a way to manage stress, anxiety, or emotional overload. **NappyCraft** aims to provide a safe, controlled in-game environment that supports this headspace through simulation play, without judgment.

 - **Supports all ages in a safe way** - Disabled and Age Regression isn't age limited and been able to do this in Minecraft makes it safe for everyone, younger users can't tell there friends about there medical problems or they can't want to tell people that need to Regress to deal with someting that is traumatic that could be anything from abuse or to health we all different. This is also the same for adults, we can't share with people we work with our medical problems or mental health, Many of us can't share with your family that we want to go back to a younger age to deal with some kind of stress or abuse in a younger life or even abuse they are living not, **YES** adults can also deal with abuse everyday and just push though, Age Regression in a game can sometimes be there only release.

 - **Supports all ages in a safe and respectful way** – Disability and age regression are not limited by age. NappyCraft provides a private, controlled environment within Minecraft where players can engage in simulation play safely and without judgment.

 - **Unable to be open to there friends or family** - Many younger players may not feel able to talk openly with friends about medical needs, or may struggle to explain why they need to regress to cope with something difficult—whether that’s trauma, stress, or health-related challenges. This mod offers a space where they don’t have to explain themselves to be understood.

- **Adults need help as well** - The same is true for adults. Many people are unable to share personal medical needs or mental health struggles with colleagues, friends, or even family. Some may use age regression to cope with stress, past experiences, or ongoing difficulties—something that can be hard to talk about openly.

For some, having a safe space like this in a game isn’t just a feature—it can be an important form of comfort, release and even lives, been able to regress is able to balance peoples feeling enough so they can cope another day.

**Within the mod, players can**:

 - Craft and wear a variety of nappies/diapers
 - Manage changing mechanics as part of the experience
 - Use items such as baby bottles that interact with the system
 - Trigger wetting mechanics either through gameplay or commands (e.g. /wet)
 
All features are optional, allowing players to engage at their own comfort level and in their own way. **NappyCraft** is about creating a space where players can feel represented, supported, and at ease—whether that’s through accessibility, personal comfort, or safe simulation play.

### Our Features
✅ Features:  
*** New Armor: We provide new armor in the style of **nappies**/**diapers**  with there own levels of buffs depending on the armor.
*** **Drinking**: Drinking our of our bottles will give you a chance to advance your wet state, once your wetness is at 6 your
armor and buffs will change.

* **Nappy/Diaper Buff control**: as of <span style="color:red;">1.0.7.3</span> the game rule **doNappyBuffs** was added so users can enable and disable **nappy**/**diaper** buffs
NappyCraft as become Regressia Compliant as <span style="color:red;">1.0.7.5</span>, please view the change logs for full information, if the mod as asked you to read the Warning Message then you came to the right place.

* **DoManualWearing** - New game rule to help make makers and server owners to make user use drink to wet if this is set to fault it will disable the <span style="color:#78311f;">/wet</span> command from working as of <span style="color:red;">1.0.7.5</span>
